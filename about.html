Who We Are

TandiseMehr is a boutique interior design studio based in IRAN-Raht. It was established in 2013 by Mehrzad and Mehdi. We have an abundance of experience curating stunning interiors, from compact apartments to family homes in Golsar. Our interiors simultaneously enhance the architecture of the space and mirror the values of our clients.

As a boutique studio, we can provide each customer with a completely personalized service that meets their every need. Our interior is designed according to the customer's needs at the forefront, executed flawlessly with the best attention to detail.

We work with our clients to bring the most beautiful views to life, with the end result of an endless haven that complements even the busiest urban lifestyle.

Our Philosophy

At the heart of our philosophy is the commitment to treat each project as if it were our own home. We share many of our customers' values, including the desire for a space with meticulous design and impeccable customer service. Our approach is uncompromising and all functional and aesthetic requirements are met.

The practical aspects of each project are carefully considered. We cleverly allocate our customer budget and spend more on the areas that matter most. All fittings and products are of exceptional quality, selected with a timeless aesthetic and at the same time truly customized.

Finally, we believe in steady progress. It's a combination of creativity, hard work and patience that has enabled TandiseMehr to transform from a small studio to a studio that successfully completes high-budget projects.

Our Style

Our work is a reflection of our customers, and we use an eclectic mix of styles to achieve a truly customized finish, including mid-century, Scandinavian, industrial and contemporary styles. However, all of our black and white signature projects support low-cost luxury. An interior that can be seamlessly integrated with modern life.

Our Founders

Since the creation of TandiseMehr in 2013, founders Mehrzad and Mehdi have been combining their skills to deliver flawless interiors that perfectly encompass the vision of their clients.

Mehrzad, our creative director, brings both creative vision and technical talent to TandiseMehr. Inspired by European art and architecture, Mehrzad's interior strikes a perfect balance of warmth, sophistication and function. He applies a fresh and open-minded approach to each project and spends as much time as it takes to create a comprehensive understanding of each client's needs.

Mehdi, our project manager and co-manager, brings coherence and order to the design process. He works closely with clients, contractors and the wider team to ensure that all milestones are met and the project runs smoothly from start to finish. With a background in project management and management consulting, paying close attention to detail helps ensure professional delivery within the client's time frame and budget.
