The TandiseMehr Process

TandiseMehr is a custom interior design studio with a wealth of experience that creates timeless interiors that enrich architecture and complement urban life. Our 5-step process has evolved over the years, enabling us to offer fully customized home interior design services from concept to completion. We build a deep understanding of our client's tastes and routines to ensure that their interior space exceeds expectations.

Over the years, we have established relationships with talented craftsmen and suppliers, enabling us to provide the best quality parts to our customers. Creating a great home requires control and coordination at every step. We have developed an internal capability for interior architecture, interior design, project management and project procurement. Each step is planned and executed inside.

Stage 1 - Concept Design

Summary: We provide a concise design that identifies both your style and application needs. Accurate understanding of your daily preferences and style allows us to carefully personalize the design of your home, and choose an interior that is perfect for both your taste and lifestyle.

Poll: TandisMehr visits your home and carefully examines the spaces. Then we create a digital model of your home and start working on the overall layout plans to suit your needs.

Style test: We create a personal style contest and set up a workshop with you and your family to create a clear vision of your future home.

Draft Budget: Once you have approved the outline, style, and scope of work, we prepare the draft budget and execution plan for a clear understanding of the costs and timeline. These documents are constantly updated throughout the project, giving you complete visibility and control.

Stage 2 - Detailed Design

Mood-boards and Workshops: Following the approved concept design, we prepare mood panel options for each space. After a few complete workshops with you, we will make the final decision on everything in the project. This may include; Loose and fitted furniture, decor, lighting, joinery, kitchen, sanitary ware and finishing. We guide you through this complex but exciting process of creating a truly personal home.

3D Visualization: We will work with our 3D artist to bring the design to life and ensure that all your new interior elements work well in the architecture of your space. This step gives you a clearer picture of your future home and allows you to make any final adjustments.

Technical documentation: After signing the plan, we will prepare and issue all technical documentation for the successful implementation of the project. This includes all technical drawings, work plans, custom case details and product specifications. At this stage, we finalize the budget so that you have full transparency of spending.

Stage 3 - Implementation

Project Management: TandiseMehr will be the only point of contact between customers and people involved in the project. From management companies to planning officers and builders. We have had close relationships with various contractors over the years, all of whom share our ethics of professional and flawless delivery.

Our project management phase includes:

  • - Preparing and maintaining an accurate project budget to ensure appropriate cost control
  • - Obtaining all required permissions to carry out the work
  • - Tendering the work and tender analysis
  • - Appointing all relevant contractors
  • - Chair meetings between appointed teams
  • - Periodic site inspection to monitor progress and resolve queries
  • - Synchronising project program with the procurement activities
  • - Building contract administration services that include: contract valuation for interim payments and management of contract variations.

Stage 4 - Sourcing and Installation

Preparation: TandiseMehr works directly with craftsmen and manufacturers to provide the best quality according to the budget and taste of the customer.

Our team takes care of everything. Negotiate the best price, order management, oversee the manufacture of custom items, shipping, warehousing, insurance, replacement of damaged items and on-site installation.

We buy products and materials at commercial prices with volumetric discounts. Our close shopping relationships with many brands and manufacturers allow us to offer products with up to 50% discount.

Our logistics model is honest and completely transparent. We will negotiate the best discounts and pass them on to you. Instead, we receive a fixed logistics fee.

Stage 5 - Styling

The final stage of our design brings your home together and makes your space unique. From houseplants to artwork, photos of loved ones and tableware, these are carefully selected decorative items that will warm your space. We can participate as much as you like at this stage, expand the artwork and functional items from our growing list of trusted suppliers, or give you space to add a sense of style.
